Our humble roots began in February, 2014 when our lead guitarist, Greg McGee, approached keyboard player/multi-instrumentalist Pat Lucey (Monkey’s Uncle), bass player/multi-instrumentalist Garrison Locke (Nathan Oliver; Route 55 Jazz Orchestra) and rhythm guitarist Hugh Willard, with the idea of getting together to jam some in Greg’s backyard barn. We had all played together for several years in our church’s Praise band, and were interested in also feeding our musical wanderlust.

We started by each suggesting favorite tunes from the 60’s through today, and then rollicking through. Soon enough we were asked to play our first formal gig, a private event in September, 2014. The crowd loved the show and our musical fates were sealed. Keith Earnshaw, rhythm guitarist, joined us for several gigs before moving on to other endeavors. Bill Baroniunas (Resurrection Blues, Old Farm Blues Band), drums, rounded out our sound in November, 2014. We’ve played numerous private events as well as charity fundraisers, occasional bar gigs, and even several Farmer’s Markets, all to great acclaim and enthusiasm.

We branched out to writing and recording our own material, including being commissioned to write/record the theme song for author Tony Bowman’s audiobook version of his award winning novel, Valkyrie: The Road. We were also commissioned to write/record a tribute song for the Southern Wake Relay For Life in 2016.
